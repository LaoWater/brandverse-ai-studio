## Audio & Voice Consistency Strategy

To maintain coherent soundtrack and voice across AI-generated segments, we use **anchoring descriptors** - specific, repeatable terms that pull from similar latent spaces:

### Voice Anchor (use exactly in all VO prompts)
```
Voice: Male, mid-30s, warm baritone, calm and confident like a late-night radio host.
Speaks slowly with intentional pauses. Slight reverb as if speaking in a large quiet room.
Tone: Wise friend, not salesman. Documentary narrator meets midnight FM DJ.
```

### Music Anchor (use exactly in all music prompts)
```
Music: Cinematic ambient electronic. 70 BPM base tempo. Key of D minor for tension,
resolving to D major for triumph. Analog synthesizers (Juno-106 style), soft piano
(felt-dampened), subtle orchestral strings. Hans Zimmer meets Tycho meets Ólafur Arnalds.
Build pattern: sparse → layered → crescendo → resolve to single sustained note.
```

### Sound Design Anchor (use exactly in all SFX prompts)
```
Sound Design: Crystalline digital chimes (high register, glass-like). Deep sub-bass
pulses for impact moments. Whooshes with granular texture (not stock swooshes).
UI sounds: soft clicks, gentle hums, subtle electricity crackle. Space: large reverb
tail, slight delay. Everything feels "floating in a cathedral made of technology."
```
