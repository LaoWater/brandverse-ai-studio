## 1. "The 2AM Founder" (Cinematic / Emotional)

**Style**: Cinematic photography, moody, relatable
**Use**: Hero image, blog headers, emotional marketing

**FULL IMAGE PROMPT**:
```
Cinematic photograph, shallow depth of field, film grain texture. A founder in their late 20s sits alone at a cluttered desk in a small apartment at night. The only light source is the cold blue glow from their laptop screen, casting sharp shadows across their tired face. They're wearing a comfortable hoodie, hair slightly messy from running hands through it.

On the desk: four empty coffee cups stacked haphazardly, scattered sticky notes with illegible writing, a phone face-down (ignored), crumpled paper balls. The laptop screen reflects in their glasses, showing multiple browser tabs. A wall clock in soft focus background shows 2:47 AM.

Through the window behind them, city lights twinkle in the distance - beautiful but unreachable, representing the world that doesn't know they exist yet. Their posture is hunched but determined, not defeated. One hand rests on the keyboard, mid-pause.

Color palette: deep blues, cold whites from screen, warm amber from distant city lights. Mood: isolation, quiet determination, the beautiful loneliness of building something that matters.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 horizontal. Style reference: Gregory Crewdson meets Apple product photography.
```

**CAPTION**: `You built something great. Why does no one know it exists?`

## 2. "The Multiverse Conductor" (Professional / Brand Hero)

**Style**: Clean digital art, brand-forward, aspirational
**Use**: Homepage hero, pitch decks, premium marketing

**FULL IMAGE PROMPT**:
```
Digital illustration with clean, modern aesthetic. A confident founder silhouette stands at center frame, arms raised in an elegant conductor's pose. They are surrounded by a semicircle of glowing hexagonal portals, each portal showing a different social platform dimension beyond:

- TikTok portal: vibrant pink and black energy, fast-moving particles
- YouTube portal: deep red glow, authoritative, expansive
- LinkedIn portal: professional blue, networking nodes visible
- Instagram portal: gradient purple-pink, aesthetic, visual
- Reddit portal: warm orange, community threads floating
- Twitter/X portal: electric blue, real-time pulses

From the founder's hands, streams of cyan light (#00D4FF) flow outward, directing content through each portal simultaneously. The content appears as elegant geometric shapes - rectangles (posts), squares (images), triangles (videos) - all flowing in orchestrated harmony.

Background: deep space black with subtle star field and cosmic dust. The founder stands on a minimal geometric platform that glows softly beneath their feet.

Lighting: rim light on founder's silhouette, each portal casts its brand color onto the scene. Overall feeling: powerful, controlled, elegant. Not chaotic - orchestrated.

Color palette: deep space blacks, electric cyan (#00D4FF), platform brand colors as accents, subtle indigo gradients.

Aspect ratio: 1:1 square for social, also provide 16:9 for headers. Style reference: Stripe/Linear design language meets cosmic aesthetic.
```

**CAPTION**: `Stop climbing. Start conducting.`

```
Aspect ratio: 4:3 or 1:1. Style reference: Wes Anderson prop design meets vintage National Geographic maps.
```

## 10. "The Invisible Founder" (Cinematic / Artistic)

**Style**: Fine art photography, emotional, striking
**Use**: Hero imagery, emotional marketing, brand story

**FULL IMAGE PROMPT**:
```
Fine art photograph with surreal element. Busy city intersection at golden hour - beautiful warm light, long shadows. Hundreds of people walking in all directions, captured with slight motion blur suggesting constant movement and life.

CENTER FRAME: A single figure stands completely still among the motion. They're TRANSLUCENT - 50% opacity, ghostly, not fully visible. People walk THROUGH them without noticing. The city flows around them like water around a stone.

The translucent founder holds something in their cupped hands - a small glowing orb of warm golden light. This is their product, their creation. It's the most solid, real thing in the frame. Mood: beautiful melancholy, potential energy, the moment before transformation.

Color palette: warm golden hour ambers and oranges, cool shadow blues, the glowing object provides warm accent light on the translucent founder's face.

Aspect ratio: 2.35:1 cinematic widescreen. Style reference: Gregory Crewdson, Erik Johansson surrealist photography.
```
