# Creators Multiverse - Image Generation Prompts

> Simple, clean visuals with clear messaging. Pleasant to watch, pleasant to read.

---

## Style Guidelines

```
Visual Style: Clean, modern, minimal. Soft gradients, plenty of whitespace.
Colors: Deep space blacks, electric cyan (#00D4FF), warm ambers, soft whites.
Typography: Sans-serif, medium weight, comfortable reading size.
Mood: Calm confidence. Not shouty. Inviting.
```

---

## Caption Writing Principles

1. **Short** - 10 words or less when possible
2. **Clear** - One idea per image
3. **Human** - Talk like a friend, not a marketer
4. **Curious** - Questions work. Statements land.
5. **Honest** - No hype. Real value.
