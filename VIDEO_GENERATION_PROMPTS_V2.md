## Audio & Voice Consistency Strategy

### Voice Anchor
```
Voice: Male, mid-30s, warm baritone, calm and confident like a late-night radio host.
Speaks slowly with intentional pauses. Slight reverb as if speaking in a large quiet room.
Tone: Wise friend, not salesman. Documentary narrator meets midnight FM DJ.
```

### Music Anchor
```
Music: Cinematic ambient electronic. 70 BPM base tempo. Key of D minor for tension,
resolving to D major for triumph. Analog synthesizers, soft felt-dampened piano,
subtle orchestral strings. Hans Zimmer meets Tycho meets Ólafur Arnalds.
```

### Sound Design Anchor
```
Sound Design: Crystalline digital chimes, deep sub-bass pulses for impact moments.
Whooshes with granular texture. UI sounds: soft clicks, gentle hums. Space: large
reverb tail. Everything feels "floating in a cathedral made of technology."
```
